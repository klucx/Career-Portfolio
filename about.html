Who Am I?

My name is Gavin Rupert. At the time of creation, I am 16 years old and a junior at Mentor High School in Mentor, Ohio. I started showing an interested in Computer Science and software development as early as a freshman in high school. I've always been someone who's interested in technology. From an early age I always questioned how things work. I loved to take apart my WII controllers and look at the circuits and wonder, "how do these work?". I decided that's what I wanted to do with my career.
I'm someone who asks a lot of questions and is big on problem solving. Some of my favorable strengths include:

  • I work well with others
  • I don’t give up on an unsatisfactory project
  • I’m very flexible, regardless of the situation
  • I am self-motivated

I also show adaptibilty in and out of the workplace, so I'm able ensure that who I am working with recieves the best version of me.
