Mobile Design Mockups

These few mockup designs were fully made in photoshop with access to tools from sites such as feather icons and Apple and Google's development toolkits.
Furthermore, all designs were made in photoshop, and they were planned to be integrated into a mobile application using Dart and Flutter. For full resolution larger images of the ones shown below, see here.

The application is called NFTy, an application functioning similarly to that of Tinder, only with trading NFTs. This project along with its designs earned first place in the SkillsUSA Local Competition, also making it to the SkillsUSA State Competition.

The project was submitted to RITE Ohio's CoolTech Challenge, where the designs recieved the following feedback from judges:

  • Interesting concept, the design examples look clean and easy to follow as well as being aesthetically appealing.- Judge Chris Chisholm
  • Good design, aesthetically appealing, streamlined UI.- Judge Chris Chisholm
  • Aesthetically, the app looks great and the images and color scheme worked well.- Judge Grady Burrows
